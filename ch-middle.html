Chapter 06
Middle Game Concepts

1. Piece Activity > Material

Don't grab side pawns with queen. A queen on a2 < a knight on f3 + bishop on c4 + castled king working together.

Rule: If winning a pawn pulls your piece from center for 2+ moves, don't take it.

2. Development Checkpoint at Move 10

Both knights out? Bishop? Castled? Yes → attack. No → develop first.

3. Control the Center

d4-d5-e4-e5 — fight for these

Knight on e4 controls 8 squares. Knight on a3 controls 4. Same piece, twice the power.

4. CCT — Checks, Captures, Threats

Before every move: scan checks, captures, threats. "If I do nothing, what's opponent's best move?" If checkmate or queen loss — deal with THAT first.
