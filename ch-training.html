The a1-h8 diagonal is the longest on the board. If opponent has Bb7 and your g-pawn is gone: Qg2 is mate. Before pushing f4, check that diagonal.

Quick Reference: 6 Training Habits

Before Every Move

1. CCT scan — Checks, Captures, Threats. 5 seconds.
2. Diagonal check — Anything pointing at f2/f7 or the long diagonal?
3. Opponent's last move — What changed? New attack? New line?

Before Every Session

4. 10 rated puzzles — Not puzzle rush. Rated. Think first.
5. Review last loss — Find the ONE move that lost. Write it down.
6. Three-Loss Rule — 3 losses in a row = close app. No exceptions.

Opening Autopilot (memorize this)

WHITE e4, Nf3, Bc4, O-O, d3 — Italian quiet. Every game.
BLACK vs e4 e5, Nf6, Nc6, Bc5, O-O — Mirror Italian.
BLACK vs d4 d5, Nf6, e6, Bd6, O-O — QGD setup.
BLACK vs weird e5, Nf6, Bc5, O-O, d6 — Default system.

Switch to 15|10 time control. The extra 10-sec increment means you can use CCT every move. At 250-350, thinking beats speed.
