The Mission

Your games show a different opening every time. This stage locks in ONE system as White and ONE as Black. Same moves, every game. Consistency builds pattern recognition.

As White — Italian Setup

1.e4 2.Nf3 3.Bc4 4.O-O 5.d3

As Black vs 1.e4 — French Defense

1.e6 2.d5 (if 3.e5, play 3...c5)

As Black vs 1.d4 — QGD Setup

1.d5 2.Nf6 3.e6 4.Be7 5.O-O

Opening Flowchart

  • Opponent plays 1.e4? → You play 1...e6 (French)
  • Opponent plays 1.d4? → You play 1...d5 (QGD)
  • Opponent plays anything else? → You play 1...e5 (Default System from Stage 1)

Your Rules

  1. Play your prescribed opening every game. No experimenting.
  2. Develop 2+ pieces before move 6.
  3. Castle by move 10.
  4. Continue all Stage 1 habits (f2/f7 check, no side pawns, Three-Loss Rule).

Per-Game Checklist

  • Played prescribed opening?
  • Developed 2+ pieces by move 6?
  • Castled by move 10?
  • Maintained all Stage 1 habits?
