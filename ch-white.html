Chapter 02
Your Openings as White

24 games as White. You almost always open 1.e4. Good — stick with it.

What Works

WHITE Petrov · 5-0 (100%)

1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nf6 — mirror knights, you dominate

WHITE Scandinavian · 3-1 (75%)

1. e4 d5 — you take, they bring queen out, you punish

WHITE Italian · 2-1 (67%)

1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Bc4 — bishop aims at f7

What's Not Working

WHITE vs Alekhine · 1-2 (33%)

1. e4 Nf6 — knight attacks your e4 pawn

In both losses you played Nc3/Nf3 and lost the thread.

Fix: Play 2.e5 — chase the knight. Then: d4, Nf3, Bc4.

WHITE vs Random e5 · 2-5 (29%)

When Black plays something odd after 1...e5, you lose. No plan.

Universal plan: After 1.e4 e5, always: 2.Nf3 → 3.Bc4 → 4.O-O. Don't deviate.
