20 Games · Defense First

The Mission

Your 50-game analysis shows 6 losses in under 10 moves. This stage eliminates those. Every game must survive past move 10.

Your Rules

  1. Three-Loss Rule: After 3 consecutive losses, STOP. Close the app. Minimum 30 minutes before playing again.
  2. f2/f7 Check: After every opponent move, glance at f2 (as White) or f7 (as Black). Is anything new pointing at it?
  3. No Side Pawns: First 10 moves must be center pawns (e/d), knight/bishop development, or castling. Zero a/b/g/h pawn moves.
  4. Default System vs Weird: Against anything unusual (1.e3, 1.b3, 1.g4), play: e5, Nf6, Bc5, O-O, d6.

Patterns to Watch

The f2 and f7 squares are the weakest points on the board at the start of the game — each is defended only by the king.

Default System vs Weird Openings — If your opponent plays something unusual, set up this formation as Black: e5, Nf6, Bc5, O-O, d6.
